About Us - Seaside Haven

At Seaside Haven, we believe a hotel is more than just a place to stay — it's a space where memories begin, where peace finds you, and where comfort and care come naturally.

Nestled between the rolling mountains and the gentle sea, our story began with a simple dream: to create a sanctuary where nature, design, and hospitality live in harmony. Inspired by the effortless charm of Sardinian coastal towns, we've designed every corner ofSeaside Haven to reflect warmth, simplicity, and quiet luxury. From sunlit balconies to thoughtfully curated interiors, each room invites you to slow down and stay present.

Seaside Haven isn't about extravagance. It's about attention to detail, genuine service, and the kind of quiet that stays with you long after you leave. Here, you're not just a guest — you're part of a place that feels like it's always been waiting for you.

We are a small, independent hotel — and we wear that with pride. Our team is local, passionate, and deeply committed to making your stay exceptional. Whether it's recommending a hidden beach cove, preparing a sunset dinner, or simply making sure your morning coffee is just the way you like it, we're here to make your time with us feel effortless.

Come experience life by the sea — where mornings are slow, evenings are golden, and every moment is made to be savored.

Our Staff - The Heart of Seaside Haven

At Seaside Haven, our people are the soul of everything we do.

Each member of our team brings more than just experience — they bring heart, care, and a shared passion for hospitality. From the moment you arrive, you'll feel it: the smiles are genuine, the service is thoughtful, and every gesture comes from a place of pride in what we've created.

We're a small team, but that's our strength. We know our guests by name. We remember how you like your coffee, what view makes you feel most at peace, and how to make your stay feel uniquely yours. Behind the scenes, our chefs, housekeepers, and concierge staff work with quiet dedication to ensure every detail is just right — so all you have to do is relax.

Our team is local, deeply rooted in the culture and rhythm of this coastline. We love sharing our home with you, whether it's through a warm welcome, a hidden beach recommendation, or a handmade dessert passed down through generations.

We are more than staff. We are hosts, caretakers, storytellers — and above all, people who love what we do.
